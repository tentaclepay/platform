## Tentacle Pay

Tentacle Pay is the infrastructure for the **agentic economy on Sui**.

AI agents need to pay and get paid without humans in the loop. Tentacle Pay ships the economic rails so agents can transact with other agents, APIs, and services natively on Sui.

## Why Sui?

Sui now offers [gasless stablecoin transfers](https://blog.sui.io/sui-launches-gasless-stablecoin-transfers/) — agents can move USDC and other stablecoins without holding a gas token, with sub-second finality and parallel execution.

**Zero fees, machine speed — the agentic economy on Sui starts here.**
